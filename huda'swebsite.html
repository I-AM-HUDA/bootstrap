OZENE DEPLTION.

Ozone Depletion. When chlorine and bromine atoms come into contact with ozone in the stratosphere,
they destroy ozone molecules.
One chlorine atom can destroy over 100,000 ozone molecules before it is removed from the stratosphere

GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming causes climate change, which poses a serious threat to life on Earth.
Humans are feeling the impact of global warming around the world as climate change brings intense droughts,
wildfires and extreme storms with heavier rainfall.

POLLUTION

Pollution is the largest environmental cause of disease and premature death.
Pollution causes more than 9 million premature deaths,
the majority of them due to air pollution.
